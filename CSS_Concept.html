Shadow

Horizontal Property

Horizontal mean how large the shadow appear on the right with positive value. With negative value, the shadow appear on the left

In this box horizontal property is 20px.

In this box horizontal property is 80px.

Vertical Property

Vertical mean how large the shadow appear on the bottom with positive value. With negative value, the shadow appear on the top

In this text, the vertical shadow is 10px

Blur Property

The blur property is higher number, the more blured shadow. Not application with the negative number.
